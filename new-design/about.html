Our Story

Trig Productions is a Barcelona based production company that specialises in creating commercial and documentary content. We create dynamic and stylised video content highlighting the stories behind the faces and brands that form this European cultural hub.

Before settling in Barcelona, Cianna Canning was the Audience Development Coordinator at New Zealand International Film Festival, Lindsay Hynds was a wildlife photographer in South Africa, and Nina Palmier-Robertson worked as an assistant producer in London. We first worked together as co-directors in collaboration with OTOXO Productions on the documentary All to Play For (Todo por jugar), after which we established Trig Productions.

In an age when customers engage most with authenticity, we use our skills as documentarians to tell the stories at the heart of your business and showcase what makes you unique. We handle all aspects of production in-house in order to facilitate smooth communication and ensure the highest quality video content, from concept to screen.

As a newly established production company, we are currently offering outstanding introductory rates for first time clients.
