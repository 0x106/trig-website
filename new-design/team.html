Originally from the UK, Cianna’s love for exploring different countries and cultures has led her to live in New Zealand, Singapore and now Spain.

​Cianna has been drawn to visual media from a young age. This started with photography and has developed into a passion for the broader storytelling options that filmmaking offers. Her background centres around both fiction and documentary film exhibition, outreach and publicity. In more recent years she has moved into production, which has involved working as an editorial assistant for a web-based documentary project and as a co-director and producer.

Lindsay’s passion for filmmaking is driven by her want to visually tell stories. She is drawn to both fiction and non-fiction as she puts character driven stories as her top priority. She is excited to explore all that Barcelona has to offer creatively with Trig Productions.

Lindsay’s love for filmmaking started at a young age and it slowly grew into the area she wanted a career in. She studied at the University of the West of Scotland and gained a BA (Hons) degree in filmmaking and screenwriting. Since then she has gone on to do a range of courses and training programmes to build on her knowledge and skills of film production. Lindsay has had experience creating independent content as well as working for STV Aberdeen.

As a co-director of Trig Productions, she is really looking forward to discovering new stories in the city and working collaboratively within the creative hub of Barcelona city.

After studying Philosophy and Theology at the University and Edinburgh, she began to pursue an interest in documentary filmmaking and storytelling, and went on to complete further training at the National Film and Television School (in the UK).

Nina has experience working with the BBC, and other independent production companies such as Magic Light Pictures, and Mozart Films in the UK.
