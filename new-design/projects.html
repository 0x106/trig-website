All to Play For (Todo por jugar)





New documentary, All to Play For, follows the journey of the 18 players that form the team FC Pol Positiu. Young men are the most at-risk demographic on the mental health compass. They have limited spaces where they can be open about their conditions, without the fear of stigma. The changing rooms, bench and pitch of FC Pol Positiu, are one of those spaces.

But health, like football, is rarely straightforward. As the season progresses, the team face struggles with both form and health, and certain members even find themselves watching from the side lines.

Can FC Pol Positiu stay on track? And can football continue to provide a source of hope and relief for the team? It’s all to play for.



Co-Produced with OTOXO Productions

OTOXO Productions is an award-winning, documentary production company, based in Barcelona. They have 10 years experience making documentaries with vulnerable and marginalised individuals and communities. They have worked with the likes of The Guardian and produced content for TV3, BTV and BCN Mes, as well as featuring in festivals across the world.
